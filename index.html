Born from necessity, perfected through adventure

Captain Saltie was born from 20 years in the Pacific - sailing, surfing reef breaks, free diving, and long solo voyages where food was often plain and repetitive.

What started as a solution to transform bland ship provisions has become the South Pacific's finest food enhancer - crafted by a seafaring family of surfers, fishermen, and mountaineers.
